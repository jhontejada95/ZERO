## Post

**The world knows how to pay after a tragedy. It still struggles to finance one that never happens.**

ZERO is a prevention-finance protocol designed to turn verified prevention into a fundable, auditable and payable outcome.

The economic case is difficult to ignore:

• UNDRR’s Global Assessment Report 2025 estimates disasters cost more than **$2.3 trillion annually** when cascading and ecosystem impacts are included—roughly 2% of global GDP.

• UNDRR also reports that **97% of humanitarian aid** went to response, reconstruction and relief, while **less than 3%** funded prevention and preparedness.

• The World Meteorological Organization reports **$9 in net benefits for every $1 invested in early-warning systems**. Just 24 hours of warning can reduce potential damage by up to 30%.

• UNEP estimates developing countries will need **$310–365 billion annually** for adaptation by 2035, versus public flows of $26 billion—a 12–14× gap.

Prevention produces measurable economic value. The market failure is that avoided harm remains difficult to verify, govern and settle. When no disaster occurs, the successful outcome often disappears from financial systems.

ZERO addresses that gap through a new artifact: the **Prevention Receipt**.

It connects the intervention, evidence, counterfactual estimate, confidence, verification, human authorization and settlement.

The protocol follows a deliberate separation of powers:

**Agent proposes. Human authorizes. Contract settles. Auditor reconstructs.**

AI agents can organize evidence, evaluate conditions and prepare decisions—but cannot release capital unilaterally. Every stakeholder receives distinct permissions.

The functional MVP combines:

• programmable wallets and x402 from @Coinbase Developer Platform;
• operational persistence in @Amazon Web Services DynamoDB;
• a public application and serverless API deployed through @Vercel;
• ZERO, approval-escrow and settlement-escrow contracts on @Base Sepolia;
• @Ethereum Attestation Service attestations;
• EIP-712 human authorization and confirmed testnet settlement.

ZERO is B2B infrastructure for insurers, resilience funds, development banks, governments and organizations financing measurable risk reduction. Revenue can combine settlement fees, enterprise software and verification infrastructure.

The premise is ambitious but precise:

**Prevention is not the absence of an outcome. Prevention is an outcome.**
